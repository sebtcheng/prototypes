Third Level Officials Portal Guide

How to register, secure, and set up your DepEd Third Level Officials Portal account.

1Go to Records Management

Open the Records Management section to begin.

2Register your account

Register using your official DepEd email address.

3Create your password

Set a strong password for your account.

4Create your passcode

Create a passcode as an additional security measure and backup password.

5Enter the Authorization Code

Input INSIGHTED-TLO as the Authorization Code.

6Log in to your account

Sign in using your registered credentials.

7Complete your profile

Fill out the required Third Level Official profile information.

8Keep your profile updated

Log in regularly to review and update your profile information.
