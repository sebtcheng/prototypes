HRMO Quick Guide

A simplified guide for HRMOs covering position setup, publication, application review, qualification checking, applicant comparison, hiring-stage movement, and final deliberation.

Important: Use the AGAP Portal as a structured workflow tool. Evaluate every application against the specific position applied for, and base every action on CSC-approved Qualification Standards, policy rules, and HR review.
A

Prepare position data

Step 1: Upload the NOSCA

  1. Log in using an authorized HRMO account.
  2. Open the NOSCA upload or position import module.
  3. Upload the applicable NOSCA file.
  4. Confirm that the system correctly extracted the item number and position title from the uploaded NOSCA.
  5. Tick the relevant item number and position title that should be added into the Vacancies List of your Division.

Step 2: Confirm publication period

  1. Check the publication period in CS Form 9 revised 2025.
  2. Confirm that the portal publication period matches it.
  3. Correct date issues before opening applications.
B

Publish positions

Step 1: Open the position for applications

  1. Select the position record.
  2. Review all details one final time.
  3. Confirm that the QS, JD, requirements, hiring purpose, and application deadline are complete.
  4. Change the position status from Closed to Open.
  5. Verify that the position appears in the applicant-facing open positions list.
C

Review applications

Step 1: Open the applications dashboard

  1. Go to the HRMO applications dashboard.
  2. Select the relevant open position.
  3. Review the applicant list.
  4. Confirm that each record shows the applicant name, position applied for, application date, submitted credentials, qualification match status, score, current stage, and review fields.

Step 2: Review applicant profile information

  • Personal information and contact details
  • Educational background
  • Eligibility, license, or certification
  • Work experience, duties, and accomplishments
  • Training and learning development records
  • Performance rating and other credentials
  • Uploaded profile documents

Step 3: Confirm position-specific details

  1. Confirm the position applied for.
  2. Confirm that the application was submitted within the deadline.
  3. Confirm that uploaded credentials are attached to the correct application.
  4. If the applicant applied to multiple positions, evaluate each application separately.
D

Evaluate qualifications

Step 1: Check document completeness

  1. Check the completeness of the documents submitted by the applicant.
  2. Unlock the scoring and assessment section only after the submitted documents are complete.

Step 2: Review documents against QS

  1. Review the applicant's submitted documents against the Qualification Standards of the position applied for.
  2. Check the education, experience, training, eligibility, license, certification, and other required credentials as one complete QS review.
  3. Add remarks for missing, unclear, mismatched, expired, or incomplete documents.

Step 3: Record the QS evaluation result

Option Use when
Pending QS Review The application is awaiting HRMO review against the Qualification Standards of the position applied for.
Excluded The submitted documents are incomplete, so the application cannot proceed to scoring and assessment.
Qualified The applicant satisfies the Qualification Standards based on the required education, experience, training, eligibility, and verified supporting records.
Disqualified The applicant does not satisfy one or more minimum Qualification Standards after HRMO review of the submitted profile and records.
E

Start Assessment

Step 1: Start applicant assessment

  1. Initially, qualified applicants will have an assessment status of Assessment Not Started.
  2. Click any qualified applicant to open the modal.
  3. Use the modal to check and view the submitted documents.
  4. Score the applicant based on the Qualification Standards of the position applied for.
  5. Once all required areas are appropriately scored, the applicant's assessment status will become Assessment Completed.
  6. When the assessment status becomes Assessment Completed, the Appoint button will be activated for that applicant.
F

Appoint Applicants

Step 1: Confirm assessment completion

  1. Once the assessment phase has been completed, print the Comparative Assessment Results.
  2. Present the Comparative Assessment Results to the Appointing Authority.
  3. The HRMO must confirm that the results have been presented to the Appointing Authority before confirming the appointment of the applicant.

Step 2: Confirm the appointment

  1. The Appointing Authority shall select which applicant to appoint.
  2. After the Appointing Authority has selected an applicant, click the Appoint button for that applicant.
  3. Confirm the appointment of the selected applicant in the portal.
