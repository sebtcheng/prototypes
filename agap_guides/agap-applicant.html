AGAP Portal · Applicant Guide

Applicant Quick Guide

A simplified guide for applicants covering registration, profile creation, vacancy review, application submission, status tracking, assessments, and final results.

Important: Keep your profile complete, upload clear documents, check every vacancy carefully, and submit your application before the deadline.
A

Create your account

Step 1: Open the AGAP Portal

  1. Go to the AGAP Portal.
  2. Select Login or Create Account.

Step 2: Register your account

  1. Enter your required personal information.
  2. Provide an active email address and mobile number, if required.
  3. Create and confirm a secure password.
  4. Submit the registration form.
B

Create your profile

Step 1: Open your application profile

  1. After logging in, go to My Profile.
  2. Start completing your applicant details.
  3. Make sure your information is accurate, complete, and consistent with your documents.

Step 2: Complete your records

  • Personal and contact information
  • Educational background
  • Eligibility, license, or certification
  • Work experience
  • Training and learning development records
  • Performance rating and other credentials, if required

Step 3: Upload profile documents

Personal Data Sheet or equivalent profile document
Work Experience Sheet or equivalent document
Transcript of Records or education documents
Diploma or certificate of graduation
Eligibility, license, or certification proof
Training certificates
Performance rating documents
Other required position documents

Step 4: Save and review your profile

  1. Save your profile.
  2. Review every section.
  3. Correct incomplete or inaccurate information.
  4. Update your profile whenever your credentials change.
C

Find open positions

Step 1: Open the list of positions

  1. Go to Job Portal.
  2. Review the list of published positions.
  3. Use filters if available.

Step 2: Review each position carefully

  • Position title, office or unit, salary grade, employment type, and place of assignment
  • CSC-approved Qualification Standards
  • Required education, experience, training, and eligibility
  • Job Description and hiring purpose
  • List of requirements and application deadline

Step 3: Check if you qualify

  1. Compare your profile against the required education, experience, training, eligibility, and documents.
  2. Review your supporting records carefully before submitting.
  3. Confirm the deadline and submit before it closes.
D

Apply to a position

Step 1: Select the position

  1. Open the position you want to apply for.
  2. Review the full details again.
  3. Select Apply.

Step 2: Confirm your profile and documents

  1. Review the profile information that will be used for the application.
  2. Confirm that education, experience, training, eligibility, and documents are complete.
  3. Update your profile first if important information is missing.

Step 3: Submit the application

  1. Review the position applied for.
  2. Review your profile and attached credentials.
  3. Confirm that the application is complete.
  4. Submit the application and wait for the confirmation message.
  5. Take note of the application reference number.
E

Apply to multiple positions

Step 1: Review other vacancies

  1. Return to the open positions list.
  2. Review other positions that match your qualifications.
  3. Open each position and read the requirements carefully.

Step 2: Submit separate applications

  1. Apply separately to each position.
  2. Confirm that your profile and credentials satisfy the specific position requirements.
  3. Remember that each application is evaluated separately.
F

Track your status

Step 1: Open My Applications

  1. Go to My Applications.
  2. Review each submitted application.
  3. Check the position, date submitted, and current status.

Step 2: Understand common results

Stage or result Meaning
Application Submitted Your application has been submitted and received by the portal.
Pending QS Review Your application is waiting for review against the Qualification Standards.
Qualified You met the minimum Qualification Standards for the position.
Disqualified You did not meet one or more minimum Qualification Standards for the position.
Excluded Your submitted documents are incomplete, so the application cannot proceed.
Assessment Not Started The assessment stage has not started yet.
Assessment Started The assessment stage has started or is currently in progress.
Assessment Completed The required assessment stage has been completed and is ready for review.
Appointed You were selected and appointed for the position.
Not Appointed You were not selected for appointment after the final review.
Applicant rule: Complete your profile first, review each position carefully, upload valid documents, and submit every application before the deadline.
