AGAP Portal · Applicant Guide

How to apply to open positions

Use this guide to register, complete your applicant profile, review open positions, submit applications, upload credentials, track your status, and respond to notices.

Apply before the deadline Late or incomplete submissions may affect your application status.
Keep documents readable Upload clear, correct, and complete files in the proper sections.
Check your email Notices may be sent through the portal, mobile notifications, and registered email.

ACreate your AGAP Portal account

Step 1: Open the AGAP Portal

  1. Go to the AGAP Portal website or application.
  2. Select Register, Sign Up, or Create Account.
  3. Read the registration reminders and privacy notices.
  4. Proceed only if you agree with the required terms and data-processing notices.

Step 2: Register your account

  1. Enter your required personal information.
  2. Provide an active email address and mobile number, if required.
  3. Create and confirm a secure password.
  4. Submit the registration form.

Step 3: Verify your account

  1. Check your registered email for a verification message.
  2. Open the verification link or enter the verification code, if required.
  3. Return to the AGAP Portal and log in using your registered credentials.

BCreate your applicant profile

Step 1: Open your application profile

  1. After logging in, go to Profile, Applicant Profile, or My Profile.
  2. Start completing your applicant record.
  3. Make sure your information is accurate, complete, and consistent with your documents.

Step 2: Enter personal and contact information

  • Full name, date of birth if required, address, contact number, and email address.
  • Use contact details you actively monitor.
  • Confirm that your registered email address is correct because notices may be sent there.

Step 3: Enter educational background

  1. Enter school name, degree or course, level completed, dates attended, and year graduated if applicable.
  2. Include honors or distinctions if applicable.
  3. Upload supporting documents when required.

Step 4: Enter eligibility, license, or certification

  1. Provide the type of eligibility, license, or certification.
  2. Enter rating, examination date, issuance date, validity date, license number, or certificate number if applicable.
  3. Upload proof or Means of Verification.

Step 5: Enter work experience

  1. Enter employer or office, position title, employment type, start date, and end date.
  2. Describe duties, responsibilities, and relevant accomplishments.
  3. Upload supporting documents when required.

Step 6: Enter training and performance records

  1. For each training, enter the title, provider, inclusive dates, hours, and topic or competency area.
  2. Upload certificates or supporting documents.
  3. Enter performance rating, rating period, issuing office, awards, outstanding accomplishments, and other credentials if required.

Step 7: Upload profile documents

Personal Data Sheet or equivalent profile document
Work Experience Sheet or equivalent document
Transcript of Records or education documents
Diploma or certificate of graduation
Eligibility, license, or certification proof
Training certificates
Performance rating documents
Other documents required by the position
Before saving, check that every file is readable, uploaded to the correct section, clearly named, and consistent with your profile information.

Step 8: Save and review your profile

  1. Save your profile.
  2. Review every section.
  3. Correct incomplete or inaccurate information.
  4. Update your profile whenever your credentials change.

CFind open positions

Step 1: Open the list of positions

  1. Go to Open Positions, Job Openings, Vacancies, or the equivalent section.
  2. Review the list of published positions.
  3. Use filters if available.

Step 2: Review each position carefully

  • Position title, office or unit, salary grade, employment type, and place of assignment
  • CSC-approved Qualification Standards
  • Required education, experience, training, and eligibility
  • Job Description and hiring purpose
  • List of requirements and application deadline

Step 3: Check if you meet the requirements

  1. Compare your profile against the required education, experience, training, eligibility, and documents.
  2. Review your supporting documents carefully before submitting.
  3. Confirm the deadline and submit before it closes.

DApply to an open position

Step 1: Select the position

  1. Open the position you want to apply for.
  2. Review the full details again.
  3. Select Apply, Submit Application, or the equivalent action.

Step 2: Confirm your applicant profile

  1. Review the profile information that will be used for the application.
  2. Confirm that education, experience, training, eligibility, and documents are complete.
  3. Update your profile first if important information is missing.

Step 3: Attach or confirm required credentials

  • Education documents
  • Work experience documents
  • Training certificates
  • Eligibility or license proof
  • Performance rating documents, if required
  • Other position-specific documents

Step 4: Submit the application

  1. Review the position applied for.
  2. Review your profile and attached credentials.
  3. Confirm that the application is complete.
  4. Submit the application and wait for the confirmation message.
  5. Take note of the application reference number, if provided.
  6. Confirm that the application appears in your My Applications dashboard.

EApply to multiple positions, if desired

Step 1: Review other open positions

  1. Return to the open positions list.
  2. Review other positions that match your qualifications.
  3. Open each position and read the requirements carefully.

Step 2: Submit separate applications

  1. Apply separately to each position.
  2. Confirm that your profile and credentials satisfy the specific position requirements.
  3. Remember that each application is evaluated separately.
  4. Do not assume that qualifying for one position means you automatically qualify for another.

FTrack your application status

Step 1: Open My Applications

  1. Go to My Applications, Application Status, or the equivalent section.
  2. Review each submitted application.
  3. Check the position, date submitted, and current status.

Step 2: Understand common stages

Stage or result Meaning
Application Submitted Your application was received by the portal.
Initial Evaluation Your documents and qualification match are being reviewed.
Qualified You met or exceeded the minimum QS and submitted complete, valid documents on time.
Disqualified You did not meet the minimum QS, even if your documents were complete.
Excluded You did not submit complete and valid documentary requirements on or before the deadline, regardless of qualifications.
Comparative Assessment You may be scheduled for WST, WE, BEI, or another assessment activity.
Shortlisted / Deliberation Your application may proceed to further ranking, review, or HRMPSB deliberation.
Selected / Hired / Not Selected A final or later-stage result has been released.

GRespond to assessment schedules and notices

Step 1: Monitor notifications

  1. Check AGAP Portal notifications regularly.
  2. Check mobile in-app notifications, if available.
  3. Check your registered email address and spam or junk folder.

Step 2: Review assessment notices

  • Assessment type, date, time, and venue or online platform
  • Materials to prepare
  • Documents to bring or upload
  • Contact information and attendance instructions

Step 3: Attend or complete assessments

  1. Follow the notice instructions.
  2. Arrive or log in on time.
  3. Prepare the required materials.
  4. Complete the assessment honestly and within the allowed time.
  5. Monitor the portal after the assessment for updates.

HReceive final results

Step 1: Check final notifications

  1. Final results may be released through AGAP Portal notification, mobile notification, or registered email.
  2. Read every notice carefully and follow the stated instructions.

Step 2: If selected or recommended for appointment

  1. Review the congratulatory notice or appointment-related instruction.
  2. Prepare the attached appointment documentary requirements.
  3. Submit required documents within the stated deadline.
  4. Follow HRMO instructions and monitor your email and portal account.

Step 3: If not selected

  1. Review the regret notice or not-selected status.
  2. Keep your applicant profile updated.
  3. Apply to future open positions for which you are qualified.
  4. Make sure future applications are complete and submitted on time.

IApplicant checklist before submitting

My AGAP Portal account is active.
My email address and contact details are correct.
My applicant profile is complete and accurate.
My education, experience, training, and eligibility records are complete.
My supporting documents are readable and uploaded to the correct sections.
I reviewed the position details and qualification standards.
I checked the list of requirements and application deadline.
I saved or noted my application confirmation.

JImportant reminders

  1. Submit complete and valid documents before the deadline.
  2. Use accurate and truthful information.
  3. Keep your profile updated.
  4. Monitor your registered email and AGAP Portal notifications.
  5. Apply separately for each position.
  6. Each application is evaluated against the specific requirements of the position applied for.
  7. A system-generated score or status may support screening, but the final process follows applicable recruitment rules and HR review.
Applicant rule: Complete your profile first, review each position carefully, upload valid documents, and submit every application before the deadline.
